About Us

All born and raised in Vail, Colorado, The Stage Frights’ four members have been together since their first footsteps. Elizabeth, The Stage Frights’ lead singer and one of two electric guitarists, started the band in 2010 when she put posters around her high school asking for people to join her and her love for music! Since their very first public concert in August of 2011, they have done what they love to do and have shared outstanding music with all of their fans!

Ryan

Ryan, the band’s base player, was the second to join Stage Frights after Matthew joined and convinced Ryan to, too.

Matthew

Matthew is the band’s lead electric guitarist, and is also the oldest out of all of the Stage Frights’ members. Though he may seem big and tough, he actually has a hobby of crocheting cute stuffed animals.

Jeremiah

Being the band’s sole drummer, he is constantly overshadowed by the singers and guitarists of the band. But, he plays a very important role of helping write the music and some lyrics of Stage Frights’ songs.

Elizabeth

Elizabeth, who is Stage Frights’ lead singer and keyboardist, also plays electric guitar in some of their songs. Being the founder of the band, she is often seen as the “boss”, a nickname also given to her by her bandmates.
